Site Plan – The Wheel of Time Fan Hub

1. Site Name

The Wheel of Time Fan Hub – This name clearly communicates the fan-driven nature of the site and connects directly with the title of the book series. It's designed to be easy to remember and reflects both discussion and content-sharing aspects.

2. Site Purpose

This site provides a central place for fans of Robert Jordan’s The Wheel of Time to discuss theories, character arcs, and lore. It features a discussion board, character guides, timeline summaries, and a newsletter sign-up for fans to stay updated on discussions and fan-created content.

3. Scenarios

  • What are some fan theories about Moiraine’s true intentions?
  • Where can I subscribe to receive updates about new discussions or events?

4. Color Schema

Dark Blue (#2b3a42): Used for headings and header backgrounds to reflect the mysterious tone of the series.

Accent Orange (#e07a5f): Used for highlights, buttons, and call-to-action elements like the newsletter sign-up.

5. Typography

Merriweather (serif) – Used for headings to give a classic, bookish feel.

Open Sans (sans-serif) – Used for body text for readability across all devices.
